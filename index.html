I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad, working on problems at the intersection of distributed inference and communication-efficient learning.

Alongside my doctoral work, I am a Senior Engineer at Qualcomm, where I focus on image and video quality for camera systems, bridging algorithmic design with real-world deployment.

More broadly, my interests lie in distributed learning, statistical inference under communication constraints, and signal processing, with applications to federated systems and imaging pipelines.
